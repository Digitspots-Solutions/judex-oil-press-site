About Us

Judex Oil Press is a leading palm oil manufacturing company based in Ire Village, Umuoji, Onitsha North. We operate both a thriving palm plantation and a state-of-the-art oil press facility, ensuring complete control over quality from cultivation to production. Our commitment to excellence has made us a trusted name in the palm oil industry across Anambra State and beyond. We combine traditional farming wisdom with modern processing techniques to deliver palm oil that meets the highest standards of purity and freshness.

Our Production Process

1. Sustainable Cultivation

Our palm plantation in Ire Village is carefully maintained using sustainable farming practices that ensure healthy trees and quality fruit.

2. Careful Harvesting

Ripe palm fruits are harvested at peak maturity to guarantee optimal oil yield and rich color.

3. Modern Processing

Fresh fruits are transported immediately to our oil press facility where they undergo efficient, hygienic processing.

4. Quality Extraction

Using modern pressing technology, we extract pure oil while maintaining its natural properties and nutritional value.

5. Careful Packaging

The finished oil is packaged in clean, food-grade containers to preserve freshness and quality.

6. Ready for You

From our press to your kitchen, delivered fresh and ready to enhance your cooking.
